For over 9 years, I was an Army medic, stationed only in Kentucky and Washington. At the age for 18, I went to college for a pre-med degree, but in order to be looked at for medical school I had to stand out. The way I wanted to stand out was to volunteer as a medic. During my first contract I was really motivated about what I was doing and signed another contract for 6 more years. I ended up in Washington state and found myself wanting a bigger challenge. I linked up with Operation Code where I have been a part of for 2 years working on APIs, back-end, and other issues that may arise. I liked it so much that I started college from scratch in computer science. My goal is to learn Machine Learning which I am very interested in and use that knowledge to create something that will better the world!
